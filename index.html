Introduction

A.C.M.E, or the Agency to Classify and Monitor Evildoers, is a shadow agency better known for seeking Carmen Sandiego without success. However, the public is unaware that A.C.M.E, with all their fancy agents exuding competency in the art of secrecy and conspirations, has been a failure since the sixteenth century. This map visualizes the agents A.C.M.E has sought without success in four different periods. Join us to explore a story of incompetence while we acknowledge some of the most infamous spies A.C.M.E has tried to capture.
